Controls:
W,A,S,D - move forward, left, back, right
F - fire missile
M- bring up menu 

Game Overview:
Ateempt to shoot as many boids as possilbe 
before the timer runs out to gain the 
highest score.
Avoid being hit by the boids as this will result
in the player losing health.
